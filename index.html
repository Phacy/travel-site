A B O U T

Who are we?

Kenya is the Canaan of everything safari, and it was with this ideology that PegaTours came into existence. A few years ago, a passionate and envisioned family of Kenyan origin had a dream. It was one that could make a difference in people’s lives, enabling them to leave footprints in the world of tourism. PegaTours was this dream. The founders of this tours and travel organization had seen a niche in the tourism safari sector and wanted to be the saving grace that filled it. This pumped in the necessary passion and drive that enabled PegaTours to take its first breath in the glorious land of Kenya in 1997.

Vision

We as PegaTours stand on a foundation that spells change in the Kenyan safari tours and travel. This is the same foundation on which our vision was forged. Vision: “To make PegaTours & Travel the heartthrob of Kenyan safaris.”

Mission

PegaTours prides itself on its mission of providing quality and professional tourism services. All this is carried out with one thing in mind; to minimize negative environmental and social impacts on both the wildlife and local communities.

Why Us

The fame that PegaTours & Travel Company’s name carries with it goes beyond the Kenyan borders. We are an organization that is revered for our world-class services, both quality, and quantity-wise. Whatever your safari yearning is, we got you. Our tours & travel guidelines ensure you get the absolute best of what a Kenyan safari world has to offer. From the glamorous glacier peaks of Mt. Kenya to the glorious pink flamingoes of L. Nakuru, all the way to the sandy coastal beaches that scream. “Luxury time!” PegaTours will have your wanderlust safari urges quenched to the tee. Additionally, our tour guides have a vast knowledge of the majestic landscapes and are familiar with all the hidden gems for the ultimate Kenyan safari experience. Are you worried about a possible communication mix up or difficulty? Well, worry, not. Our PegaTours guides and drivers have a vast grasp of an array of languages. So be it good morning/ “Buenos Dias”/ “Bonjour”/ “Ohayogozaimasu”/ “Guten Morgan, ” and plenty more languages, PegaTours is home for all. Try us today.

Social Influence

Our vision to leave a mark in the tourism industry comes blended with our desire to impact a change in our communities. With this in our minds and hearts, we as PegaTours thrive to first and foremost preserve the rich cultures found within the nation. We achieve this by ensuring the purchase of local cultural artifacts, which aids in providing a source of income for the native men and women. Moreover, most of our employees are Kenyan youth. This way, we help provide opportunities for the (as the netizens call them) “hustlers” out there. And this is what we as PegaTours & Travel are most proud of.

How we achieve Our Missions

PegaTours is the “ironman” of the Kenyan safari universe not only because it has revolutionized the industry but also in its efforts to snap away from the negative impacts that wildlife face each passing day. This is done in various ways, such as wildlife conservation. As a tour company, PegaTours ensures that all of its vehicles have dustbins. It is a brilliant idea as it aids in the avoidance of littering, particularly while in the parks. This way, the animals are not subject to any form of pollution, and their habitats remain as naturally as their creator intended. Besides, once you take up this fantastic opportunity of picking PegaTours as your trusted Kenyan guide, you will notice that we avoid loud music during our tours. This helps reduce noise pollution, and you can enjoy the musical tunes of the birds and swaying tropical trees.

Lake Nakuru is one of the Great Rift Valley Lakes. It has an outstanding elevation of 1754m and a surface are of approximately 68 square kilometres. Situated south of Nakuru town in the rift valley, the lake is engulfed and bordered by the vast plains of Lake Nakuru National Park.

The freshwater lake is part of the Great Rift Valley, renowned for its must-visit touristy spots and magnificent landscapes.

Travel with Us to Amboseli N. Park 5 October 2022

Amboseli often ranks at the top of the list of national parks in Kenya. It is renowned for its excellent variety of wildlife and the best view of Africa’s highest mountain, Kilimanjaro. The park is home to four of the big five: lion, leopard, elephant, and buffalo. However, Amboseli is sought after for its free- roaming elephants and great scenic beauty.

The Ultimate Haller Park (Mombasa) Tourist Guide: Location, fees, activities, accommodation, more 5 October 2022

Haller Park is one of the most visited tourist attractions and parks in Mombasa city. It is located at Kilindini, Mombasa County, and was founded by Dr. Goerg Haller in 1926. The park is one of the oldest wildlife conservation facilities in Kenya and has grown to become one of the largest wildlife reserves on the East African coast.

The Great Wildebeest Migration in Kenya: Everything You Need to Know 5 October 2022

Also known as the Great Migration; the annual wildebeest migration in Kenya is documented as one of the must-see Seven Wonders of the World. It is one of the world’s most spectacular natural phenomena that happens at a specific time of the year. The magnificent event showcases millions of wildebeests accompanied by hordes of zebra, gazelles, impalas among other grazers moving from the Serengeti in Tanzania into the adjacent Maasai Mara Reserve in Kenya.
